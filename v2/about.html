About

Danielle Cruz (“Dani”) is a Filipina-American designer and developer. Her work primarily involves building technologies that make experiences more equitable and more accessible — particularly for learners and people with disabilities. In general, she is driven by a desire to actualize the types of tools and tell the types of stories she’d like to see in the world.

Dani graduated from Stanford University with an MS in computer science (human-computer interaction) and a BS in symbolic systems (learning). She now works as a software engineer at TeachFX.
